Visitors of Singapore

Project Objectives:

Countries that are sending the most tourists to Singapore

Proportion of tourists coming to Singapore against total outbound residents of the country

Comparison of expenditure and income patterns of these tourists

Drill-down on the exact expenditure items

Other tourism components include expenditure on airfares on local transportation (2015), Singapore-based carriers, port taxes, business, medical, education and transit visitors (2013 - 2015).

Key Findings:

This data discovery process of Visitors to Singapore between 2011-2016 has shown that there is an overall increase in expenditure, and the positions of the top few visiting countries remain unchanged. Furthermore, the trend does indeed show that Asian countries occupy a significant proportion of visitors to Singapore.

Conclusion:

STB's strategy of targeting Asian countries has proven successful. Given that the general profile of visitors to Singapore have higher spending power as noted by their expenditure pattern, STB should target its marketing efforts towards higher value recreational activities that such tourists are willing and able to spend on.
